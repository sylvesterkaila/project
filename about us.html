Company Overview


We Provide Outstanding Financial Services

Arilyd & Associates services are based on best practices derived out of experience and benchmarked on a continuous basis to ensure that we consistently deliver the very best from our services. Our aim is to provide high quality services in Bookkeeping, Accountancy services, Tax management, Accounting System Set up and monitoring as well as consulting to the public and private sector firms in Kenya and beyond through professional staff, appropriate technology to enhance growth, sustainability and add value to the business of our clients. Our vision is to be the leading provider of choice of customized professional business solutions in Bookkeeping, Accounting System set up, Tax Management, and Internal Auditing.

Company Information

Arilyd and associates Kenya Certified Public Accountants The firm Arilyd and Associates was established in 2017 and has since grown rapidly both in terms of size and the range of services offered. There are four main services division handling audit, business advisory and Tax services. Our detailed product portfolio has over 13 areas of specialization. Our services are all intended to assist clients to undertake and run a successful business development initiative in Kenya and other countries of East and Central Africa. We provide quality audit, accountancy, business development/training and management consultancy services in the East African region and beyond. We believe that in the business diversity, technology and fierce competition, the importance of high quality accounting and management consultancy cannot be over-emphasize. Investors and client needs to have confidence with their auditors, accountants and consultants that financial statement shows a full and accurate picture of their organizations performance and position and that they are receiving sound advise for business development and management. We are well structured and suited to provide services and advice that are internationally consistent and of the high standards to meet clients' expectation. The firms specializes in the following services:

~ External Audit
~ Outsourced accountancy
~ Internal audit
~ Tax Consultancy including tax compliance and exploration of tax planning opportunities
~ Business development services
~ Payroll services
~ Human resource services
